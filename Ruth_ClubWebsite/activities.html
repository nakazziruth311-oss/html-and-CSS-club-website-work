ACTIVITIES

Below Are The Activities That The Red Cross Club Has Taken Part In

Blood Donation Drive

12 March 2026

The Red Cross Club held a blood donation drive on the 12th of March 2026 in which 120 units of blood were collected and handed over to the Mukono Region Red Cross Society. This blood will be used in the aiding of sick patients in public hospitals who may be in need of blood.

Treasure Hunt

24 June 2026

In a bid to collect money for the children with cancer at Mulago Hospital, the red club council organised a treasure hunt in which each student who wished to participate paid five thousand shillings.The total amount collected was one million shillings.This money is going to be used to cater for the needs of the children with cancer.
